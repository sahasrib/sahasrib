Diabetes mellitus is a chronic (long-lasting) health condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. There are three main types of diabetes:

  • Type 1 Diabetes ‚Äì Autoimmune, requires insulin daily, usually in children and young adults.
  • Type 2 Diabetes ‚Äì Most common, often linked to obesity and inactivity, may require lifestyle changes and medication.
  • Gestational Diabetes ‚Äì Develops during pregnancy, risk factor for Type 2 diabetes later.

Common Symptoms:

  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst and hunger
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow healing of wounds

Risk Factors:

  • Overweight or obesity
  • Family history
  • Age over 45
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • High blood pressure or cholesterol
